LEO

Leo (also marketed as Leo: Bloody Sweet) is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language action thriller film[3] directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj and produced by S. S. Lalit Kumar and Jagadish Palanisamy. It is the third instalment in the Lokesh Cinematic Universe [4] and stars Vijay as the titular character, alongside Trisha, Sanjay Dutt, Arjun Sarja, Gautham Vasudev Menon, Mansoor Ali Khan and Mysskin.
